About Our School

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Established in 1995, Bright Horizon Public School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Delhi. Affiliated with CBSE, we provide a nurturing environment where students discover their potential and develop into confident, responsible citizens.

Our state-of-the-art campus, experienced faculty, and innovative teaching methodologies ensure that every child receives personalized attention and world-class education. We believe in the holistic development of our students, balancing academic rigor with extracurricular activities.

Our Academic Programs

Comprehensive CBSE curriculum designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and holistic development

Primary

Classes I - V

Foundation years focusing on basic literacy, numeracy, and environmental awareness.

  • English, Hindi, Mathematics
  • EVS, Computer Science
  • Art, Music, Physical Education

Middle

Classes VI - VIII

Building strong foundations with structured learning across all subjects.

  • Science, Social Science
  • Sanskrit (Optional)
  • Skill-based Subjects

Secondary

Classes IX - X

Board examination preparation with comprehensive subject coverage.

  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • History, Geography, Civics
  • IT/AI Courses

Senior Secondary

Classes XI - XII

Specialized streams preparing students for higher education and careers.

  • Science (PCM/PCB)
  • Commerce with/without Maths
  • Humanities Stream
